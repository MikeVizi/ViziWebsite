Last updated: 1st Oct, 2017

Get Going!

When you first log into Vizi, we point out the key features on every page and walk you through examples of using these features. This guide can be used to help with the learning process and answer any further questions you might have. There is also the Using Vizi guide, which goes through everything in more detail.

User Interface

Logging in

Log into your account at 'vizi.school' or click the login button on the 'vizi.education'. Select the 'Remember me' checkbox to speed things in the future.

Sidebar

The sidebar runs down the right of Vizi and allows you to quickly navigate between features. Your sidebar will update as you are added to groups and collections so you have access to more data.

NavBar

The navbar appears at the top of a page when you have more options of a page. For example, if you are on a group page you can view your markbook, dashboard and students. Keep an eye on the navbar to fully explore features in Vizi.

Links

Nearly everything in Vizi is a link. If you think you should be able to click on it, you probably can. This allows you to move between pages and find information as quickly and intuatively as possible.

Adding assessments

Adding assessments

Entering marks and recording data is the heart and soul of Vizi. The more data is recorded the more you can get out of Vizi and the easier it becomes to see trends and patterns in your data. To record your marks you create assessments. An assessment is anything for which you have recorded performance, so could be some homework or just the behaviour of your students in a class.

New assessments can be added by selecting one of your groups in the sidebar. This will take you to the group homepage, you then select the markbook in the navbar. This will show all your previous marks for that group and give you the option to add a new assessment! Fill in the details and you can start recording marks.

Add marks

Once an assessment has been created you can record your marks. Go ahead and enter your marks and comments and click 'Update Marks'. These marks will now be included in your markbook, your dashboard and Vizi will analyse them and start to give you feedback when it spots something interesting!

Looking at your data

Timeline

When you go to your homepage, or the homepage of any of your groups you'll see a timeline. The timeline shows information relating to all of your students or students specific to a particular group. This information is generated by other users and by Vizi itself.

Dashboards

Your dashboards offer a portal into all the data collected by you and other teachers on your students. You can view your dashboards by selecting a collection, group or user and selecting dashboard in the navbar. Then, you can do all sorts with your data, create charts, filter data, look at data from every which way, rearrange your dashboard to suit you and ultimately use it to draw some insight.

They're great for showing off at parents evening, you can see all the assessments, topics, subjects for you student, drill down and display areas of stregnth and weakness.

You can find out more on how to use your dashboars in the User Vizi Guide.

1 Exporting data

Exporting all this data is as easy as a click of the button. At the bottom of every dashboard there is a table showing the data that is in your dashboard. This table is dynamic and as you add elements and filter your dashboard it will update to show exactly what is being shown in the dashboard. Use the buttons at the top of the table to download the data in your desired format.

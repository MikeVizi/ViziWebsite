    Vizi goes beyond the conventional markbook and integrates time saving and productivity improving features to make more of the markbook.

    Calendar


    Vizi's calendar displays your timetable, events and tasks. It can link directly with your MIS to show timetables across your whole school. The calendar is highly customisable, you can filter and apply custom colours to highlight specific groups or event types.

    You will receive notifications of upcoming classes, events and tasks. Vizi's calendar is integrated throughout the platform, certain actions, such as setting an assessment, will automatically create a reminder on the calendar.

    Data Access

    Vizi gives you the ability to access data for any student or group with consummate ease. You can even create custom groups to show data by department, subject and more.

    Bespoke permissions can be created to allow users to track and receive feedback on specific students, groups or subjects.

    All data can be exported quickly and easily in various formats, including MS Excel and PDF.

    Share resources


    Vizi's timeline gives you a live newsfeed of information relating to your students' performance, calendar events, tasks and assessments.

    The timeline also allows you to share useful resources, links and news across your school or to specific groups or users.

    Tasks


    Vizi's task management system allows you to create and manage tasks. Tasks can be linked to assessments, timetables and events.

    You search your tasks, filter them and tick them off once they are complete. You can share tasks with groups of teachers to act as reminders.

    Class Layout


    Vizi allows you to view and edit class room layouts with a simple drag and drop interface using student's profile images.

    This makes organising new classroom layouts simple and allows you to quickly navigate between students to view their latest performance information.

    Student Info


    Vizi stores student information in a simple and searchable format. Student information cards show a student's profile picture and their key personal information. This also links to their performance data that has been recorded in Vizi to easily associate social information with performance.

    A school can choose to record different levels of student data, expanding on the default fields to record the desired level of detail.

    Guardian information


    You can store and quickly access information on students' guardians. Vizi allows you to quickly send emails and contact a guardian.

    Like all of Vizi, this can be linked to your MIS, so all your information is kept up to date.

    MIS integration


    Integrating with your current MIS couldn't be easier. A simple interface allows you to seamlessly link data from your MIS with Vizi, so it is always up to date.

An Introduction from the founders of Vizi Education

20th July 2017 | Mike Williams & Chris O'Mahony



Greetings! A warm welcome to Vizi Education. Here at Vizi we are embarking on a journey to improve outcomes in schools through data analytics.

We founded Vizi in 2016 having worked in the data analytics arm of a professional services firm for several years. Through building up experience across multiple fields, we began to consider where data analytics has yet to used to it's full potential, and where it could really make a difference. This, coupled with many discussions with frustrated teachers on the shortfalls of their performance reporting options, led us to the development of Vizi.

We have bound together the principles of intuitive data entry, good data structure and insightful visualisations to create one elegant software solution. We believe this will make the lives of teachers less arduous when it comes to recording marks and take the time and effort out of interpreting these marks to improve outcomes. Vizi will spot trends, relationships and student potential that may previously have gone unnoticed.

We will use this blog to plot the progress and development of Vizi as a product and the greater adoption of data analytics in schools. We will also seek the views of education professionals and data analytics experts to provide some entertaining and insightful posts.

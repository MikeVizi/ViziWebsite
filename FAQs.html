FAQs





What is Vizi?



Vizi is a platform designed for schools to easily collate, report and draw insight from performance data. It allows teachers to truly understand their students through data. Vizi provides the transparency and accuracy to identify both students who excel, and those who may be struggling, allowing support to be provided in the crucial early stages.

A simple and smart user interface enables teachers to intuitively and efficiently record performance data. By combining school wide data, Vizi provides unparalleled data transparency, facilitating both academic and pastoral tracking. Dynamic visuals empower the user to interact with and understand the data. Vizi generates its own intelligent real-time feedback, helping users understand the value behind the data.

Why Vizi?


Vizi goes far beyond simply recording marks and creating markbooks. It records marks that are comparable and connected. So rather than there being silos of data by teacher, by department or year, your data is all in one place, all ready to be analysed and all accessible to the right people.

Vizi gives you tools to unlock your data, notice trends and allow intervention at the right time. With the latest in interactive dashboards to display and understand your data, you can better allocate resources based on performance, understand the impact of technology at your school and much more (take a look at our 'Do more with data section'.)

We describe Vizi as an 'Intelligent Markbook'. This is because Vizi generates automated feedback based on your markbooks, looking for trends in student performance and prompting you to take action when performance dramatically shifts.

Getting Vizi


Getting Vizi is simple and fast. All you have to do is click the 'Get Vizi' link, send us your details and we will get in touch. You can then sign-in with all of your school's information in place and begin enjoying what Vizi has to offer.

All your tuition, support and guidance is at your fingertips, with built in user walkthroughs and interactive user guides. The simple and intuitive user interface makes navigating around Vizi and using it's features as easy as possible. We can also come out to your school and hold training sessions to kick-start the rollout across your school.

Where does Vizi keep my data?

Data protection is a priority at Vizi. We have built a state of the art architecture in the cloud, designed to ensure high performance and resilience. We partner with Amazon Web Services (AWS) - the world's leading cloud provider, to globally offer a secure, high-performance, load-balanced architecture.

Vizi uses end-to-end HTTPS and IP restrictions to ensure your data is always encrypted and only accessible to authorised users.

Can it work with current systems?

Vizi can be used as a standalone MIS system (see our 'features' section) or compliment your current system. There are several options to get your school's data into Vizi and make sure it remains up to date with any other systems you might use. Get in touch to find out more.

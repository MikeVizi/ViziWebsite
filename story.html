The team

Chris O'Mahony

Chris O'Mahony

Chris has spent most of his working life creating data visualisation solutions for all sorts of companies and organisations. From the NHS to our favourite high street retailers, Chris has created solutions to improve productivity and outcomes by utilising the latest in data analytics and data visualisation technology.

Spurred on by conversations with teachers about their struggles to properly analyse their data, Chris turned his attention to building a one-stop shop for teachers to store, analyse and get insight from their performance data.

Mike Williams

Mike Williams

Mike has worked with public sector bodies like County Councils, the NHS and the MOD to implement data driven solutions for problems like spiralling care costs, hospital mergers and even Brexit!

Mike teamed up with Chris to help transfer these exciting new solutions to teaching and education. For Mike, it is about giving teachers real-time data to spot student issues early, so that the appropriate intervention can be made, ensuring every student can reach their potential.

Sarah Cull

Sarah Cull

Sarah has worked as a secondary school Physics teacher for over 7 years and is passionate about using technology to improve outcomes for her students.

Sarah's desire for more immediate and better ways to keep track of her students' progress, including question by question analysis of their mock exam results, was a driving factor in the foundation of Vizi. Her continuining involvement with Vizi has kept the software true to teachers' needs and priorities.

    Humans are a visual species, we are adapted to spot trends and patterns. Even now, human pattern spotting abilities outmatch those of computers in many fields.

    Using good data visualisation allows you to understand and retain information quickly, pick out anomalies and identify trends in your data. It can help to explain complex ideas and bring large datasets to life!

    Vizi has been designed by data visualisation specialists so that teachers can get more from their data. Vizi displays all of your data in dashboards, which are simply collections of interactive visualisations. They are designed so that you can quickly and easily view all of your marks, grades and general information.

    Like all good data visualisation, the dashboards are intuitive and insightful. They are built with deep visualisation knowledge, experience and best practice - no 3D pie charts in sight!

    Take a look at Vizi dashboards in action and understand the power of good data visualisation.

    Having good data visualisation uncovers insights in your markbook you might never expect.





    Using good data visualisation allows you to understand and retain information quickly, pick out anomalies and identify trends in your data. It can help to explain complex ideas and bring large datasets to life!

    Vizi has been designed by data visualisation specialists so that teachers can get more from their performance data. Vizi displays all of your data in dashboards. They are designed so that you can quickly and easily view all of your marks, grades and general performance information. Like all good data visualisation, the dashboards are simple, yet insightful - no 3D pie charts in sight!

    With minimal effort and in real-time, you can understand and ask questions of your data. For example, you could see the students that are excelling or struggling across your year group, house or subjects. Identify the time of the day your students are performing best. Visualise the effect of different teaching methods on your students’ performance. Immediately observe the impact that classroom tech is having on performance. And that is just the start! Having powerful data visualisation at your fingertips can open up avenues you won’t even have considered.

    Insight at every level

    When you search Vizi for a student or a form, set, house, year group, subject... your data is all there, displayed in a Vizi dashboard. Vizi will generate these dashboards based on the data you have entered, showing you the key information relating to your students.

    Having the tools to quickly and effectively visualise your data saves a huge amount of time. You no longer have to rely on complex Excel workbooks or your resident data expert. It is all there, ready for you to use!.

    Create your own

    You can create, modify and save your own Vizi dashboards to tell your own story. By adding charts and graphs, you can show different performance metrics, show your data by topic or assessment type.

    Move and resize charts and graphs on your dashboard to create a view that suits you. Showing off student performance at parents evening or displaying whole school performance to regulatory bodies has never been easier.

    How is Vizi's visualisation different?





    Vizi revolves around getting insight from normally untapped data in your markbook and making it truly accessible to every teacher, making the markbook a day to day resource.

    Vizi's dashboards are dynamic. So often you see a static chart or graph that only shows data from a single perspective. Vizi allows you to explore your data and create different views and perspectives all from the same dashboard. Take a look at our video to see more.

    Vizi dashboards display your data in real-time, so you can monitor performance and make a difference when it really matters. All your teachers store their data in the same place, in the same format and have the same tools to understand their data. This empowers teachers to use their markbook as an everyday resource, one that can be used to improve teaching, not just to retrospectively judge performance.

Store and share resources for your groups and assessments.

All your resources conveniently organised in one place

Simply attach resources to your assessments to begin building up a bank of useful resources for you and your school. Whether it be a mark scheme, useful video links or lesson plans, Vizi will store and organise them for you.

Other users that share groups, topics and more can access these resources for their own use.

Share resources throughout your school

Vizi resources are designed to make sharing and developing school resources an easy and rewarding process. By adding resources to assessments you build up a bank of resources to save you time preparing for lessons.

You can search on key words to find resources colleagues have used in past and copy or adpat these resources for you own need.

Colloborate to create and save time

Creating an open, well organised and easily searchable repository for all of you or your school's resources promotes the sharing and consistency of teaching.

So often creating new lesson plans is duplictating work you or colleagues have done in the past. Having Vizi resources makes finding past resource simple.

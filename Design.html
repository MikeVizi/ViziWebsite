The founders

Chris O'Mahony

Background

Chris graduated with a degree in Physics and masters in Volcanology from Bristol University and has spent most of his working life creating data visualisation solutions for all sorts of companies and organisations. From the NHS to our favourite high street retailer, Chris has create a solution to improve productivity and outcomes by utlising the latest in data analytics and data visualisation.

Spurred on by conversations with teachers about their struggles to properly analyse their data, Chris turned his attention to building a one-stop shop for teachers to store, analyse and get insight from their performance data.

Chris enjoys fishing and scaring pigeons in the park.

Mike Williams

Background

Mike graduated with a degree in Natural Sciences from the University of Birmingham and completed an accountancy qualification whilst working in financial modelling for a consultancy company. Mike then began working with public sector bodies like County Councils, the NHS and the MOD to implement data driven solutions for problems like spiralling care costs, hospital mergers and brexit!

Mike teamed up with Chris to pursue the transfering these exciting new solutions to teaching and education. For Mike, it is about giving teachers real-time data to spot potential and difficulities so that the appropriate intervention can be made and every student can reach their potential.

Mike enjoys boxing, tennis and tending for his pet stick insects.

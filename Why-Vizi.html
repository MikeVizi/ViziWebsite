Why Vizi?

Start using for free!

Vizi helps you connect with students and parents, manage resources and calendars, but above all, it helps you make better educational decisions to impact student development. Here's why...

Making student data an everyday resource

How often do you look through your student's past performance or conduct? Weekly? Termly? Yearly? Why not every day? The answer for most teachers is time, there simply isn't enough. But with Vizi, this becomes possible.

Our aim is to make data an everyday resource, that doesn't require a huge investment of time. Vizi will look across students, subjects, years and more to spot patterns and correlations. It will then deliver insightful and easy to understand feedback in real-time.

More on getting insight

Connecting your data

Vizi allows you to see school, subject, year, form and set wide data... and it's all in the same place, all in the same format. Isn't that excellent.

Every teacher can see their students, their subjects and their groups, be that set, year group or house group! And of course, Vizi is available from any device, anywhere in the world.

Saving your time!

Save your time for the important things. Vizi is designed to be intuitive out of the box, allowing you to quickly and easily analyse your data. Every teacher can see their students, their subjects and their groups, be that set, year group or house group!
Timesaving features like auto-grade, quick fill and assessment breakdowns take the pain out of recording your marks.

Pre-empt peformance changes

Vizi's primary aim is to help you improve student outcomes... It does this by providing you with real-time information about your students' performance that you may not otherwise have noticed.
Vizi uses machine learning to spot when trends are appearing, both positive and negative, allowing you to act and impact.

Let the right people know

Vizi creates an environment where form teachers, heads or subject, years can be get information and receive alerts for students.

Changes in students performance, behaviour or attendance changes it can be a sign of important issues that need to be addressed. Parents and guardians can also view their child's data and be aware of any changes.

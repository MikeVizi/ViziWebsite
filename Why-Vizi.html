Why Vizi

Markbook

Vizi's markbook is designed to save you time with it's super simple data entry, the ability to reuse past assessment structures of your own and your fellow teachers and automatically grade assessments using custom mark schemes.

Vizi will add details to your assessments, like topic, assessment type and time of the day to enrich your markbook for future analysis.

You can quickly share marks with other teachers and view marks for your students that might have been graded by other teachers. Create multiple target grades for students and their subjects to make sure they are hitting their targets. And of course, record your marks from any of your devices.

Vizi creates a marking environment for your teachers where you can view the markbook for any student in a consistent, standardised format. All of your grading structures are set centrally by your school to maintain consistency in marking.You can choose from conventional grading structures (1-9, mastery etc.) or create your own bespoke structures to suit your school.

Keeping your marking data consistent makes it super easy to present performance information to parents and governing bodies.

Dashboards

Vizi dashboards were built by data visualisation specialists to give you the ability to easily monitor, understand and draw insight from your performance data, without a huge investment of time. Vizi dashboards make it easy to discover new trends and correlations from your markbook, see the effect of school initiatives and properly understand student performance.

Vizi automatically creates these dashboards, displaying all your students and groups in real time. You can quickly view performance by subject, topic, assessment, average grades and more, all in the same place!

Vizi dashboards puts your school's data in the right hands, allowing you to view real-time data quickly and simply at every level, be it by department, by house, gender etc.

Having this oversight at your fingertips in a format that is so easily comprehendable allows you to use your data as a resource, to make decisions and dictate the allocation of resources and funding and do this in real-time before performance is truly impacted.

Feedback

Vizi will analyse your markbook every time you submit performance data and look for trends and anolmolies in your data. For example, it will flag students with improving or decreasing performance across your groups.

This sort of feedback is designed to pick up fluctuations and correlations that you may not normally notice in the everyday. By reminding you of past performance or performance relative to peers, it can indicate when a student is struggling with a particualar topic, is having social issues in your school or maybe has the potential to move to a higher set.

Vizi delivers this feedback in real-time so you can react to students' performance when it is most relevant.

Vizi will share feedback on students' performance with other teachers that have an association with that student. This can drastically increase the transparency of student performance - creating more oppurtunities for teachers to react to changes in performance and intervene when necessary.

Vizi also provides periodic feedback on higher level performance, like subject performanc or yearly performance to paint an overall picture of school progress.

School Management

Vizi integrates the core markbook, dashboard and feedback features with other productivity tools to help you stay on top of your workload.

This includes a school wide calendar that can be viewed by teacher, group and student and displays your timetable and key deadlines. A homework diary, task manager, class room layout tool and student information database.

Vizi can act as a school database, with a simple search function allowing you to look up student details and contact information. You can quickly view timetables for your teachers and students.

Vizi can link with your current MIS system so that all your current information appears in Vizi (in our nice friendly format of course!). And it is all securely cloud hosted, making impletation as easy as pie.

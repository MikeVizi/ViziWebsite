    Vizi creates a simple, standardised markbook for use by individual teachers or across the whole school.

    A simple markbook

    We believe recording marks shouldn't be a burden, we want to make it as painless as possible. So, Vizi's markbook has been designed to be simple and intuitive using a conventional and recognisable markbook format, paired with several time saving productivity tools to speed up data entry.

    Vizi automatically creates a markbook for each group you teach and allows you to flick between your markbooks, entering marks when you have the time.

    Quickly record your marks

    Entering marks is quick and easy on every device. Simple dropdowns and input boxes are used to enter data based on the grade types you have selected. Add optional comments and a status to show whether the work was on-time or late.

    Automatically grade your work

    Vizi will automatically grade your marks based on a mark scheme you have selected. You can create and save mark schemes for future use and use mark schemes other teachers have created.

    Markbooks in one place

    You can easily view all of your markbooks for your different groups in one place. Vizi gives you key details on each markbook, like completion and number of assessments recorded for that group allowing you to easily fill in the gaps. Neatly arrange your markbooks on the your markbook screen to get everything in order, quickly access those that you use a lot and keep others tucked away for later.

    Colloborate to complete

    You can colloborate with other teachers to mark work for your groups. You can view, edit and complete assessments other teachers have set for your groups.

    Create different types of assessment

    formative or summative assessments, for whatever level of detail you want to capture, you can create an assessment.

    For each assessment you can specify multiple grade types to capture, assign topics and give details and resources. Vizi will also set reminders for marking the assessment based on set and due dates.

    For an assessment you can record multiple levels of detail. For example, if there is a particularly important assessment, you can record marks for the individual sections or questions (or anything else!) for that assessment. Vizi is designed to be totally malleable to your needs - allowing you to capture lots of detail, or a broad summary of performance and behaviour.

    Create bespoke grade types and keep consistency

    In Vizi you can create any number of grade type. From the conventional, to totally bespoke to suit your marking environment. And the great thing about creating these grade types, they always remain comparable. So you may have two different grade types measuring attainment and you retain the ability to plot those two on the same graph and compare students that have been graded with these two grade types.

    Examples of grade types that we often see are - Attainment (A-F), 1-9 (1-9), Application (Very good-Poor), Mastery, 1-9, Creativity and much more. You can use any of these grade types for your assessments, but all your users are restricted to these grade types to maintain consistency across your marking environment.

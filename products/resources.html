Simply attach resources to your assessments to begin building up a bank of useful resources for you and your school. Whether it be a mark scheme, useful video links or lesson plans, Vizi will store and organise them for you.

Share and receive resources throughout your school

Vizi resources are designed to make sharing and developing school resources an easy and rewarding process. By adding resources to assessments you build up a bank of resources to save you time preparing for lessons.

What is ?


Vizi is a platform designed for schools to easily collate, report and draw insight from performance data. It allows teachers to truly understand their students through data. Vizi provides the transparency and accuracy to identify both students who excel, and those who may be struggling, allowing support to be provided in the crucial early stages.

A simple and smart user interface enables teachers to intuitively and efficiently record performance data. By combining school wide data, Vizi provides unparalleled data transparency, facilitating both academic and pastoral tracking. Dynamic visuals empower the user to interact with and understand the data. Vizi generates its own intelligent real-time feedback, helping users understand the value behind the data.

So, what are the real benefits of using Vizi?

Vizi | Features

Features

Explore the Vizi features in greater detail.

Markbook


Vizi's markbook allows a user to easily create and edit assessments from any device with a single, secure online login. Vizi collates every mark so that a user can view markbooks for all of their sets in one user friendly environment. Vizi stores data using a standardised marking structure so that all data is comparable. Teachers have the ability to create assessments, record multiple levels of performance for each student and reuse past assessments to maintain consistency across the whole school.

Vizi comes with common grading systems out of the box, but also allows users to create their own bespoke grading structure. This flexibility allows Vizi to capture data for any grade system (i.e. SATs, GCSE etc..) Target grades can be associated with students and subjects to ensure students are meeting expectations.

Visual analytic capabilities are built into Vizi's markbook, allowing teachers to spot patterns and trends relating to students and assessments.

Data Visualisation


Data visualisation allows you to combine human flexibility, creativity and background knowledge with the storage and processing capacity of Vizi to gain insight into your performance data. Using Vizi's advanced dashboards, teachers can directly interact with powerful data analysis capabilities, allowing them to make well informed decisions.

Vizi creates personalised interactive performance dashboards for teachers, subjects, sets, forms and students.

Vizi allows you to view data from every perspective, at every level and drill down into your data to identify trends and gain invaluable insight. A user can add charts to show different aspects of performance, resize and drag elements to build up custom dashboards, and save them for later use. Dashboards can be exported and printed for use in reports and performance reviews. The raw data that makes up the dashboard can also exported with a click of a button for use elsewhere.

Calendar


Vizi's calendar displays a user's timetable, events and tasks. The calendars is highly customisable, allowing a user to filter and apply custom colours to highlight specific sets or event types.

Users will receive notifications of upcoming classes, events and tasks. Vizi's calendar is integrated throughout the platform, certain actions, such as setting an assessment, will automatically create a reminder on the calendar.

Data Access

Vizi allows a user to access data for any student, set, form and subject that they have the relevant permissions for. Teachers with larger areas of responsibility, such as department or subject heads, can view performance data for their entire remit.

Bespoke permissions can be created to allow users to track and receive feedback on specific students, sets or subjects. Teachers who have permission to view certain students' performance data will also be able to include them within their performance dashboards, as well as receiving timeline updates for that student.

All data can be exported in various formats, including MS Excel and PDF.

Data from Vizi can be used to aid the prediction of future grades and ensure consistency of marking across your school.

Feedback


Students with improving or decreasing performance are flagged in real time. This brings to attention trends that may have otherwise gone unnoticed and gives form teachers information about their students when it is most relevant, rather than being restricted to end of term reports.

Real time information keeps form tutors engaged with the academic progress of their form, this increases transparency and can alert teachers to potential pastoral concerns.

Periodic feedback is also delivered on group performance, including general trend information.

Interactive Timeline


Vizi's timeline gives each user a live newsfeed of information relating to their calendar, events, tasks and assessments.

The timeline displays notifications on student performance to prompt intervention or recognition. Vizi uses data analytics to identify trends in performance data, giving users the most valuable feedback in a simple and intuitive manner.

The timeline allows teachers to share assessments, links and news across their school or to specific groups or users.

Tasks


Vizi's task management system allows users to create and manage tasks. Tasks can be linked to assessments, timetables and events. They can also be searched, filtered and ticked off once complete.

Tasks can be shared with groups of teachers to act as reminders.

Class Layout


Vizi allows the user to view and edit class room layouts with a simple drag and drop interface using student's profile images.

Student Info


Vizi stores student information in a simple and searchable format. Student information cards show a student's profile picture and their key personal information. This also links to their performance data that has been recorded in Vizi to easily associate social information with performance.

A school can choose to record different levels of student data, expanding on the default fields to record the desired level of detail.

Time Saving


Vizi is designed to make your life as easy as possible (when it comes to recording and getting insight from your data, that is). Vizi includes a quick fill function that allows you to fill in multiple grades at once and quickly mark by exception. The auto-grading function allows you to create custom mark schemes to automatically fill out grades based on marks that you enter.

Vizi has an extremely simple and intuitive interface allowing you to record and extract information with just a few clicks. You can access Vizi from any of your devices and it always remains in that same friendly and easy to use format.
